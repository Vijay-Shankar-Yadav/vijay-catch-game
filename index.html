About Our Village

Mahmmadpur Ke Mathiya is a peaceful and vibrant village located in the heart of Bihar. Surrounded by lush green fields, ponds, and mango trees, our village is known for its simplicity, unity, and rich cultural heritage.

The people here live harmoniously and celebrate all festivals with joy and togetherness. Education and farming are the main focus of most families, and new development projects are helping modernize our community.
